== Description ==

<p><strong>Shop Badges</strong> is a WooCommerce badge designer for WordPress that helps you display sales and discount badges more beautifully & attracts more customers. Create the perfect product badges with custom texts, images, and icon labels to sell your products on the fly. Replaces the default WooCommerce product badges with event sales, special discounts, offers, special features, unique products, and more. To increase conversion shop badges helps you more than ever you find before.</p>

<p><strong>Shop Badges</strong> plugin is built for sellers who want to display their specialized products using custom & unique badges. Can also show discontinued or sold-out badges on products that are no longer available. Enable you to use custom texts with your chosen background.</p>
